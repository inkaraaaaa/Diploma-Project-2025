Tips for Students

Your Guide to Growing Professionally and Finding Your First Internship

Where to Start?

Don’t panic if you’re unsure about your future path yet — that’s normal in your first years. Here’s how to start:

  • Reflect on your favorite subjects or activities
  • Try online quizzes (like Google Career Certificates)
  • Attend fairs, talks, and events
  • Talk to mentors or counselors
  • Try different roles via clubs or volunteering

✅ Tip: You don’t need a final goal right away — explore and experiment!

Where to Find Internships?

Start your search with platforms like:

  • hh.ru — filter by “no experience”
  • LinkedIn — check internship posts
  • Telegram channels — find local student communities

🗣️ Networking tip: Message companies directly — startups love proactive students.

What to Prepare Before You Apply?

  • Resume: 1 page, include skills, education, projects
  • Portfolio: GitHub, Notion or Docs for examples
  • Profiles: LinkedIn, hh.ru

📁 Pro tip: Keep all documents in a ready-to-use folder

How to Present Yourself?

Prepare answers to:

  • "Tell me about yourself"
  • "Why are you interested in this role?"
  • "What are your strengths and weaknesses?"

Use examples from your life, smile and show enthusiasm!

Useful Resources to Learn and Grow

  • Stepik.org — free tech courses
  • Coursera.org — free options available
  • YouTube — try Joma Tech for tech careers
  • Leaders of Digital — hackathons, competitions

💡 Bonus tip: Join a community on Discord, Telegram, or Reddit
