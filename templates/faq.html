What is InternGo?

InternGo is a platform for students and young professionals to find internships, job opportunities, and career advice.

Is InternGo free to use?

Yes! Registration, job search, and access to educational resources are completely free.

When can I start looking for an internship?

You can start searching for internships as early as your first or second year of university. Early-stage internships focus on learning, while paid opportunities are more common in the third or fourth year.

How can I apply for an internship?

Click the "Apply" button on a job listing and upload your resume. If the employer is interested, they will contact you.

Who can use InternGo?

InternGo is open to students, graduates, and entry-level professionals looking to gain experience and build their careers.

How do I search for internships on InternGo?

Simply register, complete your profile, and use filters based on field, location, and job type.

How can I create a resume without work experience?

Include academic projects, hackathons, online courses, and volunteer experience. You can use InternGo's Resume Builder for a professional layout.

Are remote internships available?

Yes, many companies offer remote internships. Use the "Remote" filter when searching for opportunities.
